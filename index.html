I am Joey Tsui

As an aspiring programmer, I started my coding journey just four months ago with a strong passion for building applications and solving complex problems. I was determined to make a career out of coding, so I decided to enroll in a 16-week bootcamp to accelerate my learning and sharpen my skills. During the bootcamp, I was fully committed to coding, dedicating an average of 80-100 hours per week to learning new languages and technologies, building projects, and collaborating with other aspiring programmers. It was a challenging experience, but also one of the most rewarding periods of my life.
